Minesweeper

Summary

Reveal tiles, plant flags, you know the drill. Uncovered tiles will show how many of the surrounding eight tiles have mines on them, if any. Right click to plant a flag on tiles you think have mines. The game is won once all tiles without mines have been revealed. Good luck!



Features

  • Multiple difficulties
  • Saving and loading
  • Explosion "effects"
